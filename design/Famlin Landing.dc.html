Get started · self-hosted

Up and running in three steps

Famlin ships as a single Docker container. Deploy it on a home server, a Raspberry Pi, or any VPS — you stay in full control of your family's data.

deploy — famlin
# 1 · grab the compose file
$ curl -O famlin.app/compose.yml
# 2 · start Famlin
$ docker compose up -d
[ok] Famlin running on :8080
[ok] Sign in with Google enabled
[ok] Ready to invite your family
Full self-hosting guide →
1
Deploy the server
One Docker container on your own hardware or VPS. Point a domain at it and you're live.
2
Invite your family
Send an invite link. Everyone signs in with Google — no new passwords for grandma to remember.
3
Get the app
Everyone downloads Famlin for iOS or Android and connects to your server. Share your first memory.
Features

Everything you need.
Nothing that distracts.

Self-hosted & open source

Your data lives on your server, never ours. MIT-licensed and auditable — no vendor, no lock-in, no surprises.

Chronological feed

Newest first, ordered by time. No ranking, no missed posts — exactly the way it should be.

Milestones that stand out

Birthdays, births and anniversaries get a warm accent card — impossible to miss, never intrusive.

Like & comment

A heart or a short reply. Warm engagement without like-counters or performance pressure.

Your photos, your storage

Add photos straight from your camera or gallery. They're stored on your own server — full quality, fully yours.

Calm, not addictive

No infinite scroll, no read receipts, no notification spam. Open it when you feel like it.

You own it all

Your family's memories never leave your control.

Because Famlin runs on your own server, there's no company sitting between you and your family. No data mining, no algorithm deciding what you see, no counters pulling you back. You host it, you own it, and you close the app when you're done.
