Get started · self-hosted

Up and running in three steps

Famlin ships as a pre-built container image on GitHub Container Registry, plus Postgres, driven by one Docker Compose file — no source checkout or build step required. Deploy it on a home server, a NAS, or any VPS — you stay in full control of your family's data.

deploy — famlin
# 1 · get the compose file (no source checkout)
$ mkdir famlin && cd famlin
$ curl -O https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TimVanOnckelen/famlin/main/docker-compose.yml
# 2 · configure & start — pulls the image from ghcr.io
$ nano .env # JWT_SECRET, POSTGRES_PASSWORD
$ docker compose -f docker-compose.yml up -d
[ok] Pulled ghcr.io/timvanonckelen/famlin
[ok] Migrations applied
[ok] Ready to invite your family

1
Deploy the server
One backend container plus Postgres, on your own hardware or VPS. Put a reverse proxy in front for TLS and you're live.
2
Invite your family
Send an invite link from the admin panel. Everyone signs in with your chosen SSO provider (Google, Microsoft, Authentik, ...) or a simple email + password.
3
Get in — app or browser
Everyone installs Famlin for iOS or Android, or simply opens your server's address in a browser. Share your first memory.
Features

Everything you need.
Nothing that distracts.

One feed, every family group

A calm, chronological timeline for one family or several — post to multiple groups at once when something belongs in more than one.

Trips — a shared travel journal

One itinerary, check-ins from every stop, photos along the way, a day counter, and co-travelers who can add to it too.

Polls for family decisions

Ask a question, everyone votes, and the tally updates live — perfect for picking a date, a name, or a restaurant.

Milestones that stand out

Birthdays and other big moments get a warm accent card — impossible to miss, never intrusive.

A group chat for each family

Optional, per-family chat for quick back-and-forth — replies, attachments, and read receipts, switched on by an admin.

React, reply, and mention

Six reactions, comments with photos of their own, @mentions that notify the right person, and favorites to save for later.

Your photos and videos, your storage

HEIC support, automatic compression, and video thumbnails — full quality, stored on your own server, not a stranger's cloud.

Link your existing photo library

Connect an Immich server or a local folder, and filter albums by recognized family members an admin maps by face.

Apps everywhere — including the browser

Native apps for iOS and Android, plus a full web app served by the same server — family who'd rather not install anything can just open a link.

Step three · however you like

Once your server is live, everyone gets in

Install Famlin for iOS or Android, or skip the app store entirely — every Famlin server also serves a full member web app at its own address, so anyone can just open it in a browser. The Android app is on Google Play. There's no single iOS listing — Apple's distribution model means whoever runs your server builds and shares the iOS app with the family; the self-hosting guide covers doing that with Expo EAS.
